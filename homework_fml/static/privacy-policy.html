Privacy Policy of Homework – Fully Merged List

As this is a non-profit student project, we don't have the resources to provide any guarantees in terms of privacy or security. Therefore, legally speaking, any data you provide to us, or data about you that we fetch from other services you provide us that access to, can't be considered to be stored privately and securely. Your data acquired in relation to this service may be accessed, used or processed in any way by anyone on and off the internet. All data is stored in ElephantSQL, therefore their Data Processing Agreement applies.

All data we store about you is strictly necessary to provide this service and it is not possible to make an exemption. In case you want all your data deleted from our storage, please email us at privacy@homework-f.ml.

However, you may read on for information about what are our intentions (which we, as stated above, don't guarantee) regarding collecting, processing and storing your data. The following text is not legally binding.

What data we collect

All data we collect about you is requested, stored and used only for the purpose of providing and improving this service.

We need your email address solely for the purpose of identifying your account and keeping a way for you to reset your password. We will never send you any emails other than to initially verify the email address, reset your password when you request it and to inform you about important events regarding your account, such as detected password breach or an issue with an integrated service.

You can enable various integrations with other services. In that case, we need to store some information to authenticate to that service to fetch your tasks. This varies from service to service, if you are interested in details, we will be happy to help at privacy@homework-f.ml.

All data imported from the integrated services is stored only for the purpose of displaying it back to you.

Who can access your data

We don't purposefully sell or automatically provide your data to anyone. However, developers of this service may occasionally look into the database at various operational data (such as number of registered users). Your data (including identifying information such as email address) can be looked at while diagnosing any issues with the service. When we authenticate you to the integrated services, we only send the data necessary to fetch your homework from them. This usually means any login credentials when you add them and access tokens from later on.

All data is stored in a PostgreSQL database located in Ireland, hosted on ElephantSQL (please see their DPA). Traffic to this application passes through the Cloudflare network, so some data about your usage of this website can be stored there too. Both these services are highly secure, however, I don't want to be held liable for any potential access credentials leak etc.

Source code of this application is available on Github, so you are free (and welcome!) to look at it to find and report any potential security and other issues.

If you have any questions or objections, we will be happy to help at privacy@homework-f.ml.
